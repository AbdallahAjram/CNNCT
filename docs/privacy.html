Privacy Policy for CNNCT

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Account Data: We collect your name and email via Google Sign-In or Email registration to manage your account.

Communications: Your messages and chat metadata are stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore) to enable your chat history across devices.

2. Sensitive Permissions

Microphone: We use your microphone only for real-time voice calls via the Agora SDK. We do not record or store your audio data.

Location: We only access your location when you use the "Send Location" feature. This is used once to generate a map link and is not tracked in the background.

3. AI Processing

Our "AI Suggester" feature processes selected messages via secure Cloud Functions (Firebase/OpenAI) to provide message suggestions. We do not use your private conversations to train AI models.

4. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time via the "Delete Account" button in the app settings. This permanently removes your data from our databases.
